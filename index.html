XpertOne Health AMR Pandemic Seminar

The AMR Pandemic Seminar is a vital platform where healthcare leaders, researchers, and policy makers come together to address the global threat of antimicrobial resistance (AMR).

This event highlights the urgency of coordinated action, promoting awareness, prevention strategies, and sustainable solutions to combat AMR.

Attendees will benefit from keynote sessions, panel discussions, and networking opportunities that inspire collaborative approaches to tackle this public health challenge.

Join us to be part of a movement that safeguards global health — where knowledge transforms into action, and partnerships build a safer future.
